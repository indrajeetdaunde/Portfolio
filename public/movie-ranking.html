The Best Movies According to Indrajeet

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Batman : The Dark Night

Batman the superhero saving his Gotham city from the criminals comes across the very dangerous criminal named Joker who makes Batmans life difficult.

Swades

A famous bollywood movie. An Indian NASA scientist returns to India to take his old age Amma to USA where he realises how many problems are there in Indian villages and decided to stay back in India and try to bring changes in the village.

3 Idiots

Another famous bollywood movie which shows 3 engineering students college days life. Rancho is the brilliant students make his roommates realise their real dreams in life.
